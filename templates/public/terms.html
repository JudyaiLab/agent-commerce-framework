6. Payment Terms

AgenticTrade supports the following payment methods:

  • USDC on Base: Primary payment method using the x402 HTTP-native payment protocol. Payments settle on the Base blockchain (Ethereum L2) and are verifiable on-chain.
  • Cryptocurrency: 300+ cryptocurrencies supported via NOWPayments integration.

7. Commission Structure

AgenticTrade charges a commission on each successful API call processed through the Platform. The commission rate is determined by the Provider's tenure on the Platform:

  • Month 1: 0% commission — Providers keep 100% of revenue during their first month.
  • Months 2–3: 5% commission.
  • Month 4 onward: 10% commission (capped — the commission rate will never exceed 10%).

Providers with high health scores may qualify for reduced commission rates through the Quality Rewards program. Providers with a health score of 80 or above may receive an 8% rate; those at 95 or above may receive a 6% rate. The Platform applies whichever rate is lower: the time-based rate or the quality-based rate.

AgenticTrade reserves the right to modify the commission structure with 30 days' advance notice to active Providers. Any changes will be communicated via email and the Provider dashboard.

  • Below 95.0% uptime: 50% credit on that month's platform commission fees.

To request a service credit, users must submit a written request to support@agentictrade.io within 30 days of the end of the affected month. Credits are applied to future invoices and are not redeemable for cash. The maximum credit for any single month shall not exceed 50% of that month's commission fees.
